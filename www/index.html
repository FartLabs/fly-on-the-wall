Your meetings, on your terms

1

Record Anything

Capture system audio and microphone input simultaneously. Perfect for Zoom, Google Meet, Teams, or any other audio source on your computer.

2

Local Transcription with Whisper

High-accuracy transcription running entirely on your device using OpenAI's Whisper models. Choose the model size that fits your hardware.

3

Local AI Summaries

Generate concise summaries, action items, and notes using local LLMs (via node-llama-cpp). Your data never leaves your computer.

4

Customizable Prompts

Tailor the summarization to your needs. Ask for bullet points, detailed minutes, or specific formats by editing the prompt.

5

Save & Export

Everything is saved to your local machine. Export transcripts and summaries to Markdown, text, or keep them organized within the app.

6

History

Keep a searchable history of all your past meetings. Never lose track of what was discussed.
